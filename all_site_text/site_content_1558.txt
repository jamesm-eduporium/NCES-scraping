SERVICES




Supporting Staff










LACOE offers a variety of services designed to help districts enhance their support of educators' wellbeing, professional development, career exploration and more.


















Credential Programs
 
A range of administrative, educational, and leadership credentialing programs for educators looking to develop their career pathways. 

















EASE
 
An assessment and brief counseling service for employees of member districts and their families.

















Professional Development
 
Providing educators access to professional development opportunities, programs and resources.
