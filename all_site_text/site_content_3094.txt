NDES Teachers & Staff

Our caring staff members make themselves available to our students to help support learning both during the school day and on selected days before and after school. The years of experience our teachers bring to the classroom is a testament to the excellent instruction your child receives every day at NDES. Please feel free to contact any one of our team members—we’re here to help.
