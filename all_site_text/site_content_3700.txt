 WJUSD Graduation Requirements WJUSD Graduation Requirements


English - 40 credits
World History - 10 credits
US History - 10 credits
Gov (5 ) & Econ (5) - 10 Credits
Mathematics (must include Math I) - 30 credits
Science (Life 10 and Physical 10 ) - 20 credits
Physical Education - 20 credits
Technology - 5 credits
Health - 5 credits
Electives - 70 credits 
 
Ethnic Studies course covered by Required or Elective course. 
 
Total - 230 credits
 





UC/CSU "A -G" requirementsUC/CSU "A -G" requirements


A- US History/ World History - 2 years
B - English - 4 years
C - Math (Math 1, Math 2 and Math 3) - 3 years
D - Lab Science - (Bio, Chem, Phys) - 2 years
E - World Language (same language) - 2 years
F - Visual/Performing Arts - 1 year
G - College Prep Electives - 1
 
"C" or better grade must be earned in all courses
UC's recommend additional years of Math, Science and World Languages
