Bio


Mr. Frank comes to us with years of experience serving students as both a teacher and an administrator. Prior to being hired as our Principal at Lyons, he was an assistant principal for 7 years at both Altona Middle School in St. Vrain Valley School district and Bill Reed Middle School in Thompson School District. Prior to being an administrator, Mr. Frank was a middle school science teacher, football, girls basketball, and high school track coach in Colorado Springs. His academic background includes a Masters of Arts in Educational Leadership with Principal Licensure from the University of Colorado, Colorado Springs, and a Bachelor of Science in Natural Sciences, Concentration in Biology Education from Colorado State University, Fort Collins, Colorado. Mr. Frank grew up along the front range and is excited to get to know the students and families at Lyons Middle Senior High School.
