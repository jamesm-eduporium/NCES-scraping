Bio


Welcome to Mead Middle School:
Mead Middle School's motto is "Success Lasts a Lifetime." Our students gain success through the high academic rigor in our core and enrichment classes and the lifelong lessons they learn through involvement in our cocurricular and extracurricular activities. This combination makes Mead Middle School one of the top middle schools in the state.
Josh Barnett, Principal
