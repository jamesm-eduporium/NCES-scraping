New to Longmont?






Flagstaff Academy is located in Longmont, Colorado, a vibrant city with a population of just under 100,000 residents. The city is part of Boulder County with 22 square miles and sits at an elevation of 4,979 feet above sea level, with stunning views of the Rocky Mountains. With more than 1,500 acres of parks and open space, Longmont is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. 
Longmont is also home to several high-tech companies and a vibrant restaurant scene. It is conveniently located 37 miles from Denver, 16 miles from Boulder, and 30 miles from Rocky Mountain National Park. The city is home to a diverse and eclectic population with recreation centers, art galleries, restaurants, shops, outdoor festivals, community events, and activities for the entire family. It is also home to the St. Vrain Valley School District, which Flagstaff Academy is a part of, and which serves over 35,000 families.
