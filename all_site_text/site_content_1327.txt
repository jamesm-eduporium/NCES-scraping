Staff Email






Staff Email Options


 
Kalispell Public Schools staff email accounts are integrated into Google Gmail.

 To access your school district Gmail account, Click Here. 

If you cannot access your email or forget your password please contact IT support -   helpdesk@sd5.k12.mt.us
PLEASE NOTE THAT YOUR PASSWORD MUST CONTAIN THE FOLLOWING:
No consecutive numbers (like 1234)
You cannot use your name in anyway in the password
You cannot use your last 24 passwords
Minimum PW Length : 8
The following are special characters and cannot be used: \ / * [ ] : ?
PW must meet complexity: At least one upper case character ( K ), one number (
8 ), one symbol ( # ) and one lower case character ( k ).
EXAMPLE: #KpassWord5  (Please do not use this password)
