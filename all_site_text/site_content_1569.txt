Welcome to Dragon Flight





We are pleased that you have chosen Dragon Flight, Flagstaff Academy’s before- and after-school care program, for your child and your family. At Flagstaff Academy, we recognize the importance of providing a quality child care experience. 
 
Dragon Flight offers before school care starting at 7 a.m., after school care running until 6 p.m., late-start, drop-in care, non-school day care, and so much more. Please visit our fees page for pricing information.
 
Purpose and Philosophy
The purpose of the Flagstaff Academy Dragon Flight program is to provide opportunities through care that will enrich the lives of children in a safe and welcoming environment, supporting them to realize their highest potential. The Dragon Flight care staff offer extended learning opportunities to enhance school achievement, enrichment, and recreation providing a well-rounded experience for the children in their care. 
 
Goals and Objectives
The primary goal of the program is to provide a safe, structured, and positive environment which allows children to be creative, sociable, and happy. Our objective is to work as a team with the school staff by offering activities that supplement class work and support the well-being of children.
