Library Reservations (Staff)







 
Need to Reserve the Library?
The library can be reserved throughout the school day.

To reserve the library:



Check the homepage's calendar for date/period availability.
Send Ms. Garcia an e-mail that includes the date(s) and period(s) you would like to reserve the library.
Ms. Garcia will send you an e-mail confirmation after placing your reservation on the calendar.
If your class needs a library orientation, please notify Ms. Garcia prior to your library visit.
