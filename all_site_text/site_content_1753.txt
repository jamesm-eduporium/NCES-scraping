What Do Counselors Do?





School counseling services:

School counselors are certified/licensed educators who improve student success for ALL students by implementing a comprehensive school counseling program. School counselors help all students apply academic strategies, manage emotions, apply interpersonal skills, and plan for career and academic success.
 
Services provided include:

Responsive Services

Individual Short-term Counseling
Small Group Counseling Meetings
Crisis Response

Psycho-Educational Lessons

Mental Health
Bullying
Growth Mindset
Self-Awareness
Self-Management
Responsible Decision-Making
Relationship Skills
Social Awareness



Career and Academic Planning
Parent and Teacher Collaboration
