Through membership in the Mesquite Education Association (MEA), employees have a voice concerning professional practices. They are also encouraged to participate actively in civic affairs. MEA members are represented by a body able to speak with authority for them in communicating with other Mesquite ISD entities as well as the community which they serve.

MEA membership benefits include collaboration, professionalism, scholarships, discounts, life insurance, and other continually improving benefits.

History of MEA





The Mesquite Education Association began in 1958 when it formed under the name Mesquite Teachers Association. Dr. Ralph H. Poteet, who later became the Superintendent of Schools, served as the organization's second president during the 1960-61 school year. The name was changed to Mesquite Education Association in 1970.

Facility Representatives





FR Meetings
Meetings are held on the last Tuesday of every month, with the exception of November, December, and May.  The FRs will receive important, fun, and money-saving information for Mesquite and the surrounding areas. You will have an opportunity to meet with Dr. Rivera and to win a door prize! Light refreshments are served at 4:00 p.m. The meetings begin at 4:20 p.m. and are held in the Boardroom at the PDC, unless otherwise notified. If you cannot attend, please make arrangements for your alternate to represent your facility.
FR questions are due by the end of every month during the school year, apart from May.

MEA Membership





Each MISD Employee who joins MEA receives the following benefits:
1. Employer paid $10,000.00 Life/AD&D Insurance Policy*
2. Legislative Action
3. Discounts for Movie Theater tickets (AMC, Cinemark, Regal, Studio Movie Grill), Six Flags, Hurricane Harbor, Great Wolf Lodge, Hawaiian Falls, Dallas Summer Musicals, Dallas Zoo, Fort Worth Zoo, Dallas Arboretum, local businesses and restaurants.
4. Scholarships
5. FREE Admission to all MISD Events (Athletic, Fine Arts, etc., excluding Varsity Football Games)
6. MEA Mailbag and MEA Discount Pages
Professional dues $10.00/year Associate dues $5.00/year
To Enroll:
If you are in an enrollment opportunity (benefits open enrollment or new hire 31-day), please sign up and designate your beneficiary(s) online in the Mesquite ISD benefits enrollment portal. If you are unable to enroll online, complete a MEA Membership/Enrollment Form and a MEA Beneficiary Form. Return both forms to your Facility Representative (FR) or bring the forms and cash or check (payable to MEA) to the MEA Office located at 2133 North Beltline Road Mesquite, Texas 75150.
MEA will issue a membership card to you. For more information, call 972-882-5504 or x25503.
*MEA members must be full-time or part-time employees, actively working 18.75 hours or more per week to be eligible for the insurance or under FML protection for benefits. Sub/hourly employees, interns and residents are eligible for MEA membership, but they are not eligible for the $10,000 basic life and/or ad&d policy.

FR Questions





FR Questions provide an opportunity for staff members to anonymously ask questions of district leadership that are of general interest to all employees. Submitted questions that are relevant only to a specific campus or situation will be referred back to the campus for attention. 

Answers to FR Questions are presented and reviewed at each monthly FR Meeting and then forwarded to all employees. Submit your FR Questions to your campus Facility Representative before the end of the month.
