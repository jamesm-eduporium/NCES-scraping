Welcome to SchoolDude, FS Direct, our online Facility Request Portal. Our “online” program for Staff is entitled MySchoolBuilding.com. Once you click on the link below it will bring you to the registration page.Click the arrow that says “Register Here”. The account number you will need to type in is 771011584. Please fill in the remaining areas of the registration form and click “Register”. You may now begin submitting your requests. If you do not submit a request when you initially sign up, your information will not be saved. In order to complete your request, the submittal password is wildcats.
