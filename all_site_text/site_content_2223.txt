Scholarships & Financial Aid







Each year the Student Services Office announces countless scholarship opportunities for students. Come by the office and get information scholarships. 
 
Questions? Call or email Mrs. Green in the Student Services Office: sherri.green@wjusd.org or 530-661-5418
 
Financial Aid: 
FAFSA and the CA Dream Act open October 01 and must be completed by March 02.
Helpful Financial Aid information can be found at CaliforniaColleges.edu and StudentAid.gov
 
        
 
Scholarships: 
Click here for our Scholarship Bulletin that gets updated frequently with new scholarships
Every college has a financial aid and scholarship section… apply for these scholarships even if you are not sure you will attend or not (if you do attend, you will be glad you applied!) 

 
 
Local Scholarship Application: Click Here
 
*It is important to remember that any scholarship or program that charges money is probably a scam. Please reach out to your student’s counselor if you have any questions.
