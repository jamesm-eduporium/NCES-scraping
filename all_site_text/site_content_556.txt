Website Accessibility Policy
It is the policy of the Board of Education (“Board”) of Marysville Public Schools (“District”) to ensure that online content provided through the District’s website(s) is accessible to students, prospective students, employees, guests, and visitors with disabilities, particularly those with visual, hearing, or manual impairments, or who otherwise require the use of assistive technology to access information.  For more information about the District’s Website Accessibility Policy (No. 9960). For questions or comments, contact the web accessibility coordinator at 810-455-6007 or by email.
