Attention New Families of Huber Heights City Schools 

Avoid The Last Minute Registration Rush
Members of our registration team will be available on July 23rd, 24th, 25th until 7pm to help complete your student's registration for the 2024-25 school year.  Please stop by our central office, located at 5954 Longford Road, for one-on-one assistance with the registration process.
To best serve you, please bring the following documents:
 - Student's Birth Certificate and/or Passport
 - Student's Immunization Record (to include name, date of birth, provider's name and address)
 - Parent's Current State ID or License and/or Passport
 - Complete Court Custody/Divorce Documents (must be signed by judge/magistrate)
 -  Proof of Residency -
a. Current Mortgage Statement
b. Current & Signed Lease Agreement (full lease must be signed by tenant and landlord/property owner)
c. Current AES Statement (full page to show service address; no final bill or disconnect)
d. Current CenterPoint Energy Statement  (full page to show service address; no final bill or disconnect).
We look forward to meeting with you and your incoming Warrior!
