Welcome to Rapid City Area Schools Staff Development
A goal of the Rapid City Area Schools is to create a culture of inquire, where professional learning is expected, sought after, and is an ongoing part of teaching and learning.
We offer classes three times a year;  Summer, Fall and Winter sessions.
Classes are open to everyone.  You do not need to be an employee of Rapid City Area Schools to take any of these classes.
 
Sign up for classes here at our Staff Development Portal
 
Classes are offered in a variety of categories:
General Knowledge
Classroom Management
Science
Math Literacy
Technology
 
Classes offered are available for various types of credit:
Undergraduate
Graduate
CEU’s
Audit (No particular reason)
 
Credits can apply towards teacher recertification
 
A fee is charged for each of the classes based upon the type of credit desired.  The fee is included in the class description.
 
For questions please contact:
RCAS Office of Teaching, Learning & Innovation 605-394-4067
 
Sign up for classes here at our Staff Development Portal
