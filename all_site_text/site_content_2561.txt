As we approach our 2024 graduation celebration, I want to take a moment to recognize the unwavering drive, strength of character, and rigorous academic preparation that our approximately 2,200 graduates have demonstrated throughout their time in St. Vrain.

Opportunity CLIMB Summer Program 

The Opportunity CLIMB program is hosting a summer program at the Innovation Center in June for SVVSD students who will be in high school next year. It will include the opportunity to explore certifications in AWS, Salesforce, and Cisco through field trips, career exploration, mentors, and time with professionals in the IT industry.
