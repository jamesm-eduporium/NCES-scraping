Bio


EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE Russell Fox was a teacher for nine years, teaching English 9, 10, 11, 12 as well as AP Seminar and Yearbook. He was a varsity volleyball coach and assistant basketball, golf, and baseball coach. After teaching, he has worked in administration at Coal Ridge Middle and Frederick High. He graduated with his Bachelors of Arts from the University of Colorado, his Masters of Science from Colorado State University, and his Doctorate of Education from the University of Colorado at Denver. OUT OF SCHOOL FUN When not at FHS, he enjoys spending his time with his family. His wife Lindsay is also an educator and his children keep him busy! Russell has been at FHS since 2019. Go Eagles
