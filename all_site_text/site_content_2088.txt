Legion Collegiate Academy

3090 Long Meadow Road
Rock Hill, SC 29730
Phone: 803-620-6040
Email: info@legionlancers.com
It is the mission of Legion Collegiate Academy to serve high school students in a small, safe, family-centered setting, who seek the opportunity and challenge of a rigorous curriculum, high academic standards, and elite athletics while earning up to two years of college credit while in high school.
