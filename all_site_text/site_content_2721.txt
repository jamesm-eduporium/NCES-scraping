Staff
We recognize the importance of our role as educators to equip and provide the students of PHS with an all-encompassing, quality education. We take care to hire individuals with a passion for teaching and the qualifications to deliver what students need. In fact, more than 85% of our faculty members have earned at least a master's degree or its equivalent.If you have concerns you wish to discuss with a member of our team, please use the contact information below to connect with us.
