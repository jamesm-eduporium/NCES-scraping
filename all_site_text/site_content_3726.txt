All Woodland Joint Unified School District students have free online access to
 Woodland Public Library Resources.
 
Username: w followed by your student ID card (lunch) # (example: w12345)
Password: last 4 digits of your student ID card # (example from above: 2345)
 
 
Woodland Public Library Resources include:
Help Now - Science and math tutoring
Mango Languages - Learn a new language
Zinio Digital Magazines - Magazines for your Kindle or iPad
Library to Go - Read a book or listen to it on your phone
Job Now - Resume writing








TExtbooks and chromebooksTExtbooks and chromebooks


Textbooks MUST be returned or paid for before transferring your student out and no later than the end of the school year.  
Chromebooks MUST be returned before transferring your student out or upon graduating high school. 
 


Parents/Guardians will be responsible for lost or damaged books/materials as well as any damages sustained to a Chromebook. Please call the school as soon as possible if your student will be transferring to another school to schedule a day/time to return  their books/materials.

Each student is issued one Chromebook and charger to be used for school assignments and school related activities. The library does not have an unlimited supply of chargers. It is the individual responsibilty of students to bring their charger with their Chromebook every day.
