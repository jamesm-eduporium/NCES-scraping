Mary Chris Blickhan






Mary Chris Blickhan has been a parent educator with the Shawnee Mission Parents as Teachers program for 6 years.  She has a Bachelor’s Degree from UMKC in Sociology and Administration of Justice, as well as a Master’s Degree in Social Work from the University of Kansas.  Mary Chris has spent the majority of her career working with children and families in various situations.  She especially loves working with families through Parents as Teachers, supporting families during the fun and important early years of their children’s lives!  She and her partner have a son in college and a son in high school and two huge, wild dogs!  
marychrisblickhan@smsd.org
(913) 993-9380                                                          






Carrie McAtee






Carrie McAtee has been a parent educator for 13 years.  She was  also a classroom teacher for 10 years.  She has a master's degree in school administration.  Carrie loves working with young families  helping them to feel confident and be the best first teacher for their children.

Emily Rodriguez





Emily Rodriguez has been a parent educator with the Shawnee Mission Parents as Teachers program since 2022. She has a Bachelor’s Degree in Spanish and Latin American History from the College of Wooster (Ohio) and a Master’s Degree in Spanish from West Virginia University. Emily worked as a classroom teacher for 10 years before arriving to Parents as Teachers. She loves supporting families during this exciting time in their children’s lives! She lives with her husband, her infant son Marcus, and her bonus teenage son, Alexander, along with a dog and cat (that surprisingly get along!). 
emilyrodriguez@smsd.org  
(913) 993-9380






Suzanne Sawalich






Suzanne Sawalich is proud to be a parent educator for the  Shawnee  Mission School District.  She is from a small town in  western Kansas.  She received a Bachelor's Degree in Education from Fort Hays State University, and a Master’s in Elementary Counseling  from the University of Kansas. Suzanne taught third and fifth grade for 10 years before becoming a parent educator in 2006. She loves working with families and making a positive difference in their lives.
suzannesawalich@smsd.org
(913) 993-9380        
 
 






Jennifer Wonderly






Jennifer Wonderly has been a parent educator with the Shawnee Mission School District since 2007.  Jennifer loves providing support, education and helping empower young families as they navigate parenting.  She received a Bachelor of Arts in Human Development and Family Life from the University of Kansas.  She also received a Master's of Science in Family Therapy from Friends University.  Jennifer lives in Prairie Village, is a wife to Sean and a busy mother to two sons, Alex and Sam, and a terrier named Wally.      
jenniferwonderly@smsd.org  
(913) 993-9380
