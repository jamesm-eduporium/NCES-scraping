Heart For OthersLeaders support those around them and cultivate positive relationships
Commitment to GrowthLeaders are unfulfilled by maintaining the status quo. They are averse to complacency.
Passion for ResultsLeaders are driven on by their own evidence of impact or that of others they lead





Leaders in Mesquite ISD display three characteristics:



Commitment to Growth
Heart for Others
Passion for Results

To guide you toward owning your leadership development, these characteristics contain eight specific competencies. Together, these characteristics and competencies define excellent leadership in Mesquite ISD.  This online resource is intended to support your personal growth and development through Mesquite ISD’s Leading Through Ownership model. Click on a characteristic in the diagram to reveal its competencies, relevant definitions and examples of those competencies in various roles throughout the district.
